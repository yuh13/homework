Wind

A loose direct correlation between wind speeds and distance from the equator seems to imply that wind speeds are higher further from the equator. With our current analysis, there is no obvious relationship between latitude and wind speed, so further analysis is needed. It is easy to see that there may be some correlation; notice that a much larger number of cities in the northern hemisphere report over 20 mph of wind.
